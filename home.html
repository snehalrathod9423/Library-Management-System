About Our Library

Our Library Management System is designed to simplify the process of managing a library's resources, helping users track books and authors, search for their favorite titles, and add new books to the collection. The system is easy to use and intuitive, whether you're a student, teacher, or librarian.

Key Features

Search Books

Easily search for books by name or author, making it quick and convenient to find your desired books.

Add Books

Admins can add new books to the system, including book names, authors, and types of books.

Manage Library

Administrators can manage the library by adding, updating, and removing books as needed.
